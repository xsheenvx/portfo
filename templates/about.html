Loreto Humberto Vazquez

Unable to focus and find my passion I decided to join the working force.

Loreto the worker Bee

I tried my hand at just about every job I could get, from landscaping and construction to fruit sorting and operating machines in packing sheds. Loreto, Programmer extraordinaire!

Remembering my passion of when I was a kid, I decided to start on my path of building my AI.
